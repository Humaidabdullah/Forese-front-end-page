What is the Mock Placements?



Mock Placements is an event organised by FORESE (the Forum for Economic Studies by Engineers of SVCE) and the SVCE Placement cell and has been its flagship event since 2006.

The event endeavours to prepare pre-final year students for the actual placements by letting recruiters from various companies interview students. Students become aware of their shortcomings and strengths and can prepare accordingly for the real placements. Previous versions of the event have seen hundreds of recruiters and students participating.

KAPITAL

KAPITAL, a mega economics managerial event, conducted by FORESE, is an annual symposium which provides a platform for budding managers all over the nation to showcase their skills. From advertising to stock trading, to testing management knowledge and of course finding the next Management Guru, Kapital does it all!

What's in it for participating recruiters?



By participating in the Mock Placements, recruiters can quickly identify top talent in the college and can provide these students with internship opportunities and pre-placement offers. Recruiters who participate would also be able to select students who perform admirably and would get the chance to interview these students during the actual recruitment process of SVCE.

This event also provides a platform for recruiters to contribute to the educational community by furnishing students with their strengths and weaknesses and providing a sense of the industrial skills necessary for these students to be employable and market-ready.
